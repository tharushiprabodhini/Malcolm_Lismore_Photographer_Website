About Me

Capturing Scotland’s Wild Beauty & Special Moments

Malcolm Lismore is a passionate freelance photographer based on the northwest coast of Scotland. He specializes in capturing the raw beauty of Scotland’s rugged landscapes and diverse wildlife. With a deep love of nature and storytelling, Malcolm’s photography brings every moment to life. From dramatic coastlines to serene rural scenes, his images capture the spirit of the mountains.

He also covers weddings, portraits, and special events with a creative and personal approach. Each photograph is carefully composed to capture the emotion and atmosphere of the moment. Malcolm’s work has been praised for its authenticity, detail, and visual impact. His keen eye for light and composition makes his photography both artistic and timeless.

Clients appreciate his calm presence, professionalism, and commitment to quality. Whether you are looking to capture a landscape or a special memory, Malcolm is ready to tell your story.
