Welcome to Malcolm Lismore Photography

Capturing Scotland’s Wild Beauty & Special Moments

Malcolm Lismore is a passionate freelance photographer based on the rugged North West coast of Scotland. Surrounded by dramatic landscapes and rich wildlife, Malcolm developed a deep appreciation for nature, which shines through in his photography. His greatest passion lies in capturing the raw beauty of Scotland’s natural world – its mountains, lochs, coastal birds, and wildlife.
Malcolm is also available for weddings, portraits, and special events, where he brings the same creativity and attention to detail to preserve life’s most meaningful moments.
